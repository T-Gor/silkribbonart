Introduction:

Welcome to my website. Here I would like to share my works painted and embroidered with silk ribbons.

My art projects usually start out from getting inspired by nature, flowers, plants. Seeing the beauty of nature, I want to capture this moment on canvas.

In the beginning I used silk ribbons, they perfectly repeat the delicate structure of the petals and leaves. I achieved embroidery realism by coloring the ribbons manually using the entire spectrum of the colour palette. The search for the effect of volume gave an impulse to the passion for drawing. I experimented a lot with different paints: oil, watercolor, gouache, acrylic. I have developed my own technique of drawing on fabric. Embroidered works are the result of many years of creative search.

My works regularly take part in exhibitions and art competitions. Many of them won prizes and were awarded diplomas.

Thank you for visiting my website, I will also be grateful for your feedback, this will motivate me to continue working and develop creativity.

About me:

My name is Alla. I live in Odesa, Ukraine and I am creating authentic works using silk ribbons and paints such as emroidered works in frame and various kinds of accessories.
